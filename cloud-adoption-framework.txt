AWS CLOUD ADOPTION FRAMEWORK (CAF)
Purpose:
Helps organizations plan and manage their move to AWS. It focuses on people, processes, and business alignment, not technical architecture.
There are 6 perspectives.


Imagine starting a company in the cloud:


🧠 Business
→ Why are we doing this? (goals, value, ROI)
👥 People
→ Who will do it? (skills, training, roles)
📜 Governance
→ What rules must they follow? (policies, compliance)
🏗 Platform
→ What do they build on? (infrastructure, cloud setup)
🔐 Security
→ How do we protect it? (identity, data protection)
⚙️ Operations
→ How do we run & maintain it? (monitoring, support)



1. Business
Focus: Business value and outcomes.
Includes:

Cost management
ROI
Business strategy


2. People
Focus: Workforce and culture.
Includes:

Training
Skills development
Organizational change


3. Governance
Focus: Control and decision-making.
Includes:

Data governance
Risk management
Policies and compliance

Important exam point:
Data governance belongs here. Data science does not.

4. Platform
Focus: Infrastructure and technical environment.
Includes:

Compute
Storage
Networking


5. Security
Focus: Protect systems and data.
Includes:

Identity management
Threat detection
Data protection


6. Operations
Focus: Running systems day-to-day.
Includes:

Monitoring
Incident response
Automation
