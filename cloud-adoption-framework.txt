AWS CLOUD ADOPTION FRAMEWORK (CAF)
Purpose:
Helps organizations plan and manage their move to AWS. It focuses on people, processes, and business alignment, not technical architecture.
There are 6 perspectives.


Imagine starting a company in the cloud:

🧠 Business
→ Why are we doing this? (goals, value, ROI)
👥 People
→ Who will do it? (skills, training, roles)
📜 Governance
→ What rules must they follow? (policies, compliance)
🏗 Platform
→ What do they build on? (infrastructure, cloud setup)
🔐 Security
→ How do we protect it? (identity, data protection)
⚙️ Operations
→ How do we run & maintain it? (monitoring, support)
